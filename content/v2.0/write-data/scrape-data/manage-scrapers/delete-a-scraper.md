Delete a scraper from the InfluxDB user interface (UI).

## Delete a scraper from the InfluxDB UI
1. Click **Data** in the navigation bar.

    {{< nav-icon "load data" >}}

2. Click **Scrapers**.
3. Hover over the scraper you want to delete and click the **{{< icon "delete" >}}** icon.
4. Click **Delete**.
